Our Philosophy

The PC hardware industry is loud, expensive, and overwhelming. PCBudget is designed to help you navigate from picking a CPU to your first system boot without overspending or creating bottlenecks.

We help you get your parts list in order with the essentials. Our algorithm cuts through the noise to build a balanced rig mapped perfectly to your wallet. Pick your priorities. Exclude components you've already got covered. Then, choose your budget.
